# Staff Counselling Services

## Project Overview
**Client:** Shafin Consulting  
**Year:** 2020  
**Focus:** Staff Support & Mental Wellbeing

## Our Impact
We provided specialized counselling services to the staff at Shafin Consultancy. This initiative aimed to improve staff morale and overall productivity by addressing individual and group wellbeing challenges.

### Program Benefits
- **Improved Morale:** Shifted organizational culture towards better staff support.
- **Increased Efficiency:** Correlated staff wellbeing with higher performance outcomes.
- **Tailored Support:** Provided targeted counseling based on staff needs.
