# Human Resource Restructuring

## Project Overview
**Client:** Teens For Christ  
**Year:** 2021  
**Focus:** HR Management & Policy Development

## Our Impact
We provided a complete Human Resource management restructuring for Teens For Christ. This project focused on aligning staffing with organizational goals and establishing clear operational guidelines.

### Key Deliverables
- **Staffing Realignment:** Optimized roles and responsibilities to improve engagement.
- **HRM Policy Development:** Created a comprehensive HR manual.
- **Operational Clarity:** Standardized procedures for improved transparency.
