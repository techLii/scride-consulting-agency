# Strategic Planning & Program Assessment

## Project Overview
**Client:** Echoes Of Mercy  
**Year:** 2021  
**Focus:** Strategy, SWOT Analysis, and Implementation

## Our Impact
We conducted a thorough program assessment and strategic planning exercise for Echoes Of Mercy. Using tools like the SCORE Board and SWOT Analysis, we helped define a clear path forward.

### Strategic Milestones
- **Road Map Development:** Prioritized goals for long-term achievement.
- **Funding Strategy:** Enhanced donor retention and identified new funding streams.
- **Implementation Support:** Provided ongoing advice to ensure the strategic plan was put into action.

## Client Testimony
> "SCRIDE gave us a clear prioritized road map. Their support led to increased funding and better donor retention. We now have a much more focused approach to our goals."
